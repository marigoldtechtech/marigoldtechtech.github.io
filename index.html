The Dentist

Dr. Arun Kumar

Personal Website: http://kumararun.com

He has been working as a General Dentist and an Orthodontist and has been a member of teaching faculty since 1995. He has more than 3000 completed Orthodontic cases to his credit during a span of 19 years. He received his DMD degree from Henry M. Goldman School of Dental Medicine, Boston University in 2015. He will be providing services in general dentistry, as well as preventive, interceptive, and corrective Orthodontics. Dr. Kumar received his Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) degree from Government Dental College, Rohtak, India in 1988, and Master of Dental Surgery (MDS) degree in Orthodontics from the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi, a premier health institute of India in 1994.
